Cookie policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet, or computer when you visit a website or service. They store information about how you use the website or service, such as the pages you visit. Cookies are not viruses or computer programs. They are very small so do not take up much space.

How we use cookies

We only use cookies to:

  • make our service work
  • measure how you use our website, such as which links you click on (analytics cookies), if you give us permission

We do not use any other cookies, for example, cookies that remember your settings or cookies that help with health campaigns.
