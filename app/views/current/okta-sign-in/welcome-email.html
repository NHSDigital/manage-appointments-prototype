Dear MYA user,

Get started

We’ve created an Okta account for you to securely access the NHS Manage your appointments (MYA).

Here’s what you need to do:

1. Activate your Okta account

You’ll receive a ‘Welcome to Okta’ email (from noreply@okta.com). Please activate the link within 7 days.

If you cannot find the email, please check your spam or junk.

2. Log in to MYA

Once you’ve activated your Okta account, log in to www.mya.england.nhs.uk using your Okta username and password.

Many thanks,
